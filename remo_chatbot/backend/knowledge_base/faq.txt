What type of milk do you deliver?
We deliver fresh cow milk directly sourced from local farms. It is not processed or mixed with preservatives.

Why do you use glass bottles?
Glass bottles maintain freshness, preserve flavor, and prevent chemical contamination. They are reusable and environmentally friendly.

What should I do with empty bottles?
Rinse them and place them outside your door before the next delivery. Our delivery partner will collect them.

How do I pause or change my subscription?
You can contact customer support or use our subscription portal to pause, skip, or modify deliveries. Changes must be made at least 12 hours before the next delivery cycle.

What if I miss a delivery?
If you are not available, the delivery is marked unsuccessful. Re-delivery may incur an additional charge.

How do I complain about milk quality?
Contact support within 4 hours of delivery. We will replace the milk if the issue is verified.

Can I get urgent delivery?
Urgent delivery depends on availability in your area. Contact support for assistance.

Is evening delivery available in all areas?
Evening delivery is available in most zones, but some areas may have restrictions for safety reasons.
